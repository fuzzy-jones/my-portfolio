About Me

Hello my name is Fuzzy Jones, while that isn't my real name, I've been called Fuzzy since the day I was born. I didn't actually know my real name until my first day of kindergarten, when my mom informed me that the teacher would call me 'Donald". I cried because I didn't want that name and so it's been Fuzzy ever since. I grew up playing a lot of golf and video games, but now a days it's way more video games. I have also been obsessed with horror movies since I was about 3 years old, and I have a growing collection of horror action figures and horror tattoos.

I am originally from a small town in South Jersey called Cinnaminson. I moved to Charlotte, NC in 2006 where I attended UNCC and received a bachelors degree in Marketing. After working in advertising and screen printing for a few years, I decided I wanted to make a career of building things. So I went back to school to become a welder. I held many certifications and worked in various power plants and refineries. Recently I decided to weld only as a hobby, but to continue a career in building things, and found my way to Web Development. I have completed and received a certificate from the UNCC coding bootcamp, where I have gained experience in (HTML5, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, ReactJS, handlebars.js, Node.js, Firebase, mySQL, MongoDB, Express, C#, Command Line, and Git.
